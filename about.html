About Me

I'm a UI-focused software engineer at RTV Engineering, where I work on real-time operational dashboards for tracking systems. Day to day, I build React/TypeScript interfaces that display live asset data, integrate with backend APIs and event feeds, and help operators make sense of complex information quickly.

I collaborate with the business side and the backend engineers on feature development, debug production behavior, and support customer demos in secure government environments. I've also done on-site work configuring network switches and validating connectivity for RTLS reader deployments—nothing glamorous, but it taught me how the full stack comes together.

I also serve as an Army Logistics Officer, where I manage equipment accountability and coordinated movement operations for a 63-soldier platoon. That background gave me a strong sense of how to operate in structured, high-stakes environments.
